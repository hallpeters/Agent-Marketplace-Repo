How workflow pricing works

📉

Spend budget decreases each time your agents invoke a skill owned by another team.

📈

Earnings increase whenever another team's agents call a skill your team owns.

🔄

At month-end, your earnings convert into next month's spend budget — rewarding teams that build reusable skills.

⭐

Best skill-builders earn more capacity. Best at reuse spend less — the model is self-balancing.
