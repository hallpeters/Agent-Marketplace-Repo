About This Marketplace

The TRATON AI governance framework

The TRATON AI Agent Marketplace provides a unified registry for intelligent agents operating across all group brands. Agents are discoverable, composable, and subject to group-wide governance policies — ensuring compliance with the EU AI Act, GDPR, and internal data classification standards.

Cross-brand data flows require explicit policy checks and, where data is classified as Confidential or above, human-in-the-loop approval from designated governance stakeholders. Every interaction is immutably logged for audit purposes.

This demo simulates the governance pipeline that runs between every pair of communicating agents, surfacing policy decisions that would otherwise happen invisibly inside an autonomous system.
