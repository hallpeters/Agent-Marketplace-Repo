Innovators Hypothesis

What we believe, and how we'll know if we're right.

Our Hypothesis
Target User
Digital Product / IT Leads at brand level (Scania, MAN, VW T&B), or other teams working digitally who need to build and deploy agents fast — without shared infrastructure.
Pain Point
Agents are siloed by brand with no standardized way to communicate, no shared governance, and no visibility into what exists elsewhere — so cross-brand optimization is blocked and teams rebuild redundantly.
Key Feature
A cross-brand agent registry with a lightweight governance / request layer that makes agents discoverable and interoperable — without requiring infrastructure consolidation.
We'll Know We're Right When
Leads who see the registry immediately identify agents they'd want to consume or workflows they'd restructure — and ask how to get access.

Problem Space & Sprint Focus

What we're solving, what could go wrong, and how we're pressure-testing it.

Pain Point

No cross-brand visibility or interoperability

Agents are siloed by brand with no standardized communication layer, no shared governance, and no way to discover what's already built elsewhere. Cross-brand optimization is blocked, and teams keep rebuilding the same capabilities independently.

Key Feature

A registry + lightweight governance layer

Make agents across Scania, MAN, and VW T&B discoverable and interoperable through a shared registry — with a request and approval flow that enforces policy without requiring teams to consolidate infrastructure or give up brand autonomy.

Biggest Risk This Sprint

We might be solving the wrong problem

The real friction may be political or organizational, not technical. If brands don't want to share agents due to trust, competitive dynamics, or internal incentive structures, a platform solution becomes irrelevant no matter how well it works technically.

Upcoming Experiment

TRATON interviews to remove the risk

We ask brand leads across the group one focused question to test whether discoverability alone unlocks value — or whether deeper blockers dominate:

"If you knew every AI agent that existed across the TRATON group right now — what would you actually do differently?"
